Notebook Manufacturer

Our Core Pillars

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To manufacture and supply superior-quality academic notebooks that meet the evolving curriculum requirements of educational institutions, while maintaining affordability and on-time delivery for every bulk order.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and largest academic notebook manufacturer in South India — known for innovation in paper quality, printing precision, and sustainable manufacturing practices that benefit students and the environment.

Our Values

Integrity in every transaction, quality in every product, and commitment in every delivery. We believe in building long-term partnerships with institutions, distributors, and students built on trust and consistent excellence.

Our Journey

A Legacy Built Over
Two Decades

From a small printing press to a modern notebook manufacturing plant, our journey reflects the dedication of our founders and the trust of thousands of institutions across Tamil Nadu.

2005

Foundation Year

Century Printograph was established in Tirunelveli with a single ruling machine, 5 employees, and a vision to serve local schools.

2008

Engineering Segment Entry

Launched specialized engineering lab record notes, opening supply relationships with 15+ engineering colleges in the region.

2012

Capacity Expansion

Expanded the factory floor, added offset printing equipment, and scaled production to over 1 million notebooks per year.

2016

B.Ed & Pharmacy Category

Introduced dedicated B.Ed record notes and pharmacy lab records, tapping into the teacher education market.

2019

ISO Certification

Received ISO 9001:2015 quality management certification — validating our commitment to consistent product quality.

2025

Present Day

Serving 500+ institutions, with 10M+ notebooks produced annually and plans for a new automated production line.
